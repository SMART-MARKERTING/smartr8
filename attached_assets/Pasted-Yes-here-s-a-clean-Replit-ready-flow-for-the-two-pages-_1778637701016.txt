Compliance edits
I would not publish blanket claims like “no appraisal required,” “no income verification required,” or “no hard credit check” unless you have lender-approved language for each one. Mortgage ads must avoid misleading statements, and specific product claims should match the actual offer. Arizona also restricts mortgage solicitation and licensing-related conduct, so keep the assistant in a support-only role, not outbound solicitation.

The safest version of your supporting copy is:

“Fast digital HELOC options.”

“Qualification and timing vary by lender and borrower profile.”

“Review lender disclosures for full terms.”

“Some borrowers may qualify with alternative documentation.”

“Not all applicants will qualify.”
